# USER MANUAL
**YatraSahayata - India Edition**

## 1. Introduction
This User Manual serves as a detailed guide for operating **YatraSahayata**, an advanced Android-based navigation application tailored for Indian roads. The system is designed to assist drivers and travelers by providing accurate routing, offline map capabilities, trip tracking, and safety features.

The application leverages **Mapbox** technology to offer precise turn-by-turn navigation, real-time traffic updates, and a comprehensive database of Points of Interest (POIs) such as fuel stations, restaurants, and hotels. It supports both online and offline modes, ensuring reliability even in areas with poor network connectivity.

## 2. System Requirements
To ensure smooth and effective usage of YatraSahayata, the following requirements must be satisfied:
*   **Device**: Android smartphone running **Android 8.0 (Oreo)** or higher.
*   **GPS**: Functional GPS sensor for real-time location tracking.
*   **Internet**: Stable internet connection for initial map setup, live traffic, and online search.
*   **Storage**: Sufficient internal storage for saving offline maps and trip history.
*   **Permissions**: Location (Fine/Coarse), Storage (for maps/receipts), and Notification permissions.

## 3. Application Installation and Setup
### 3.1 Installation
1.  Download the **YatraSahayata APK** and install it on your Android device.
2.  Launch the application from your device menu.
3.  **Permissions**: On first launch, grant the required permissions:
    *   **Location**: Allow "While using the app" for navigation.
    *   **Notifications**: Allow to receive turn-by-turn instructions in the notification shade.

### 3.2 GPS Setup
*   Ensure **Location Services (GPS)** are enabled on your device.
*   If GPS is disabled, the app will prompt you to enable it for accurate positioning.

## 4. Main Interface (Dashboard)
The main screen provides quick access to all core features:
*   **Map View**: Real-time display of your current location and surroundings.
*   **Search Bar**: "Where to?" input for finding destinations.
*   **Category Chips**: Quick search for **Home**, **Restaurants**, **Gas**, **Hotels**, and **Coffee**.
*   **Action Buttons**:
    *   **Recenter**: Snaps the map back to your current location.
    *   **Traffic**: Toggles real-time traffic overlay.
    *   **Navigation**: Access to Saved Places and Route Planning.

## 5. Navigation Features
### 5.1 Searching for a Destination
1.  Tap the **Search Bar** at the top of the screen.
2.  Type the name of a place, address, or city.
3.  Select the correct destination from the suggested results.
4.  The map will center on the destination and show a **Route Preview**.

### 5.2 Category Search
1.  Tap a category chip (e.g., **Restaurants**, **Gas**) at the top of the screen.
2.  The app will search for relevant places near you.
3.  Results are displayed as markers on the map and a list at the bottom.
4.  Tap a result to view details or start navigation.

### 5.3 Route Preview & Selection
1.  After selecting a destination, the **Route Preview** sheet appears.
2.  View route details: **Distance**, **Estimated Duration**, and **Traffic conditions**.
3.  Multiple route options (if available) are shown on the map (e.g., neon blue for the primary route).
4.  Tap **"Start Navigation"** to begin driving.

### 5.4 Active Navigation
During the drive, the screen transforms into a driver-focused interface:
*   **Turn-by-Turn Instructions**: Visual and voice-guided directions for every maneuver.
*   **Live Updates**: Speed, Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA), and Distance Remaining.
*   **Rerouting**: Automatic rerouting if you miss a turn.
*   **HUD Mode**: Tap the "Head-Up Display" icon to mirror the screen. Place your phone on the dashboard to reflect navigation onto your windshield (ideal for night driving).
*   **Night Mode**: Toggle specifically for low-light conditions to reduce glare.

### 5.5 Logging Expenses
1.  During navigation, tap the **Menu** button.
2.  Select **"Log Expense"**.
3.  Choose **Fuel** or **Toll** and enter the amount.
4.  This cost is saved to your Trip History for future reference.

## 6. Offline Maps
YatraSahayata allows you to navigate without an internet connection.
### 6.1 Downloading a Region
1.  Navigate to the **Offline Maps** section from the menu.
2.  Tap the **Download** button (+).
3.  Pan and zoom the map to select the area you want to save (e.g., your city or travel district).
4.  Tap **Download**. Use Wi-Fi is recommended as map files can be large (~100MB+).

### 6.2 Managing Offline Maps
*   View list of downloaded regions.
*   See storage usage.
*   **Delete** old regions to free up space.

## 7. Trip History & Statistics
Track your driving habits and travel data.
### 7.1 Viewing History
1.  Open the **Trip Statistics** dashboard.
2.  View a list of all completed trips with **Date**, **Distance**, **Duration**, and **Cost**.

### 7.2 Trip Details
*   Tap on any trip to view the specific route taken on the map.
*   Analyze costs (Fuel + Tolls) for that specific journey.

## 8. Saved Places
Bookmark frequent destinations for one-tap navigation.
### 8.1 Adding a Place
1.  Search for a location.
2.  Tap **"Save Place"**.
3.  Assign a category (e.g., **Home**, **Work**, **Favorite**).

### 8.2 Using Saved Places
*   Tap the **Home** chip to immediately route to your home location.
*   Access **Saved Places** from the main menu to select other favorites.

## 9. Troubleshooting
*   **"Waiting for GPS..."**: Ensure you are outdoors with a clear view of the sky. high accuracy location mode is enabled in Android settings.
*   **"No Internet Connection"**: Check your mobile data/Wi-Fi. If offline, ensure you have downloaded the map for your current area.
*   **App Crash/Freeze**: Restart the application. If the issue persists, clear the app cache in Android Settings.

## 10. Privacy & Security
*   **Local Data**: Critical application data (Offline maps, Trip history) is stored locally on your device using an encrypted database.
*   **Location Privacy**: Your location data is used strictly for navigation services and is not shared with third parties without consent.
